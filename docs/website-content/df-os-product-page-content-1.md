Section 1: The Product Idea

Run Your Factory Like a Digital System

Factories are complex, interconnected environments. Production, quality, maintenance, safety, utility, warehouse, procurement, workforce, audits, and leadership reviews do not operate in isolation but most factory systems still do.

Df-OS brings these fragmented operations into one connected Digital Factory Operating System.

It helps manufacturers move from paper, Excel, WhatsApp, disconnected dashboards, delayed reports, and manual follow-ups to real-time visibility, automated workflows, structured factory memory, and AI-ready operational intelligence.



Section 2: What Df-OS Does

One Operating Layer for Factory Operations

Df-OS digitizes and connects the daily execution of the factory from shopfloor workflows to factory-management processes.

With Df-OS, manufacturers can:

Digitize manual logbooks, checklists, forms, audits, inspections, and approvals

Automate operational workflows, task assignments, notifications, and escalations

Monitor real-time data through dashboards, reports, and control-tower views

Capture deviations, assign ownership, trigger RCA/CAPA, and track closure

Connect existing ERP, MES, QMS, PLC, SCADA, IoT, machines, and manufacturing software

Enable mobile-first execution through Android, iOS, and web applications

Continue shopfloor data capture with offline mode in low-connectivity environments

Standardize and scale factory processes across lines, plants, departments, and suppliers

Build structured factory memory for AI-ready decision-making



Section 3: Factory Memory

Because Factories Cannot Become Intelligent Until They Can Remember

Every inspection, deviation, breakdown, approval, escalation, quality check, safety observation, maintenance action, production event, audit finding, and improvement action carries operational knowledge.

But in most factories, this knowledge is scattered across registers, spreadsheets, individual memory, disconnected dashboards, and messaging groups.

Df-OS turns daily factory execution into structured factory memory.

It captures what happened, where it happened, why it happened, who acted, what action was taken, what was delayed, what was escalated, and how similar situations were handled before.

This creates a live operating record of the factory — the foundation for real-time control today and AI-ready manufacturing tomorrow.

Factory Memory Enables:

Faster root-cause understanding

Better deviation history

Stronger audit readiness

Reusable improvement actions

Traceable decision records

Pattern recognition across recurring issues

AI-ready operating context for Vish AI



Section 4: Core Product Capabilities

Process Workflow Automation

Digitize and automate workflows across departments, approvals, checklists, tasks, deviations, inspections, audits, handovers, RCA, CAPA, and operational reviews.

Real-Time Dashboards & Reports

Track factory health through live dashboards, KPI reports, exception views, department-level analytics, and leadership-ready control-tower visibility.

Notifications & Alerts

Trigger alerts for missed checks, abnormal readings, overdue actions, downtime events, deviation risks, compliance gaps, maintenance issues, and safety observations.

Deviation Workflow

Capture deviations, classify severity, assign ownership, initiate RCA/CAPA, monitor closure timelines, and maintain a complete digital trail for audits and reviews.

Escalation Matrix

Create role-based and rule-based escalation paths so the right issue reaches the right person at the right time based on severity, timeline, department, and ownership.

Integration with Manufacturing Systems

Connect Df-OS with ERP, MES, QMS, PLC, SCADA, IoT platforms, machines, sensors, cameras, and other manufacturing software to create a unified operating layer.

Offline Mode

Enable shopfloor teams to continue capturing data and completing workflows even in low-connectivity areas, with sync support when connectivity is restored.

Mobile & Web Applications

Support operators, supervisors, plant teams, leadership users, and corporate teams through user-friendly Android, iOS, and web applications.
